QA Team Leader Learning Pathway
Each QA Team Leader follows a structured learning pathway with 3 progressive levels: Master (5 courses), Execute (5 courses), and Elevate (10 courses). Each level must be completed within 3 months from the date the TL started in their current role.

💡 TL Innovation | Process Improvements & COE Contributions
Yearly Target: 4 points total
Point Values:
High 4 pts Med 3 pts Low 2 pts
📋 Activity Definitions
H High Impact
Lead/Sole Creator of new process redesign or automation tool OR COE Lead Contribution (new SOP, KB, FAQ, or standardized templates)
M Medium Impact
Major Participation in new process redesign or automation tool OR Major Update to existing COE resources (Playbooks, SOPs, etc.)
L Low Impact
Adoption of new process or automation tool OR Minor Update to existing COE resources
✏️
How to Fill: Enter the number of activities, not points. The system will calculate points automatically.
Example: 1 High + 2 Low = 4 + 2 + 2 = 8 points total
📊
Tracking: Innovation points are tracked monthly and cumulated yearly. QA TLs must achieve a minimum of 4 points by year-end to meet their Innovation KPI target.
💬
Comments Required: When not exempt, you must provide comments explaining innovation activities OR why there was no innovation this month.
If all counts are 0, explain why (e.g., "Focus on operational stability this month" or "Team onboarding period")
